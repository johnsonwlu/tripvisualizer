# Budget Breakdown

## Flights
- Round trip from US: ~$900–$1,400 depending on timing
- Book 3–4 months in advance for best rates

## Accommodation
- Tokyo: ~$80–$150/night (mix of hotels + capsule hotel for fun)
- Kyoto: ~$100–$180/night (ryokan for at least 1 night)
- Osaka: ~$70–$130/night

## Transportation
- JR Pass (14-day): ~$500
- IC card (Suica/Pasmo) for local transit: load as needed
- Airport transfers: ~$30 each way

## Food
- Budget $30–$50/day (very doable with convenience stores + ramen spots)
- Splurge meals: set aside ~$100 total

## Activities
- TeamLab Borderless: ~$30
- Day trips (Nara, Hakone, etc.): variable, ~$20–$60 each

## Total Estimate
- ~$3,500–$5,000 for two weeks all-in (solo)
