# Potential Destinations

## Tokyo
- Shinjuku, Shibuya, Harajuku
- Asakusa and Senso-ji Temple
- TeamLab digital art museum
- Akihabara (electronics + anime district)

## Kyoto
- Fushimi Inari Shrine (thousands of torii gates)
- Arashiyama bamboo grove
- Gion district (geisha area)
- Kinkaku-ji (Golden Pavilion)

## Osaka
- Dotonbori entertainment district
- Osaka Castle
- Namba and Shinsaibashi shopping

## Day Trips
- Nara — deer park and Todai-ji temple
- Hakone — Mt. Fuji views and onsen
- Hiroshima + Miyajima Island
