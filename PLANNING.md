# Japan Trip Planning

Initial brainstorming for a spring 2026 Japan trip.

## Goals
- Experience cherry blossom season
- Visit major cities: Tokyo, Kyoto, Osaka
- Try authentic Japanese cuisine
- Explore temples, shrines, and local neighborhoods

## Rough Timeline
- Spring 2026 (March–April window)
- ~2 weeks total

## Notes
- Book accommodations early for peak cherry blossom season
- JR Pass vs individual tickets — need to research
